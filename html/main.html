Providing an Outstanding
Salon Experience

We are D'Ametri's, a family owned and operated Aveda concept salon founded in 1995 through the combined passion of Ame and Dennis Metcalfe. Our goal is to serve our guests with an outstanding salon experience and we invite you to stop in and enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of our beautiful new space located in historic Olde Town Arvada, Colorado.

Our team is a carefully chosen blend of passionate and talented professionals who have a desire to learn and grow every day. We strive to provide advanced knowledge of hair care, specialized technical skills, and personalized client communication to ensure your satisfaction. We look forward to seeing you!

American Board Certified Haircolorists

D’Ametris Aveda has three American Board Certified Haircolorists on staff. To be truly competent, capable, and creative in hair-coloring, a stylist needs much more than what is offered in school or from a manufacturer's classroom. To become certified, candidates must successfully complete a day-long, three-part examination that effectively measures their skills and recognizes a high level of competence and excellence in haircolor. Feel free to request a stylist with this certification when you make your appointment.

For more information on The American Board of Certified Haircolorists, please visit www.haircolorist.com.

Education and Training

As salon owners, Dennis and Ame prioritize education. Dedicated to lifelong learning, they are educators for a national haircolor manufacturer and have both become American Board Certified Haircolorists to create the level of quality that they encourage their entire team to aspire to. They also share their passion with other salon professionals by teaching at the annual National Haircolorist Conference in Los Angeles.

Their latest accomplishment is creating Advanced Consultation Experience (A.C.E.), a program for advancing the development of salon professionals. Click here for more information.

D’Ametri’s joins with Aveda to bring you the best in service and products. We share common values towards our environment, customer care and education. We are proud to be a part of the Aveda network with the top level of haircolorists in the nation. Ame & Dennis Metcalf, Owners
