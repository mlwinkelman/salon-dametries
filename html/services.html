Customized Consultations

Adult haircut services include a customized consultation, complimentary stress-relieving treatment, shampoo, condition, blow-dry, style, and finishing touch.

Aveda Color

Aveda's color is 93% naturally-derived and has no carcinogenic effects, unlike most other hair colors. Because they contain vegetable and mineral-based dyes and essential oils, our dyes are good for hair, coating it with essential oils to nourish it so your hair is even healthier afterward than it was before you had it colored.

Extensions

D’Ametris Aveda has three American Board Certified Haircolorists on staff. To be truly competent, capable, and creative in hair-coloring, a stylist needs much more than what is offered in school or from a manufacturer's classroom. To become certified, candidates must successfully complete a day-long, three-part examination that effectively measures their skills and recognizes a high level of competence and excellence in haircolor. Feel free to request a stylist with this certification when you make your appointment.

For more information on The American Board of Certified Haircolorists, please visit www.haircolorist.com.

Policies

Cancellations: We do understand that changes in your schedule may occur. As much advanced notice for rescheduling or canceling is greatly appreciated. D'Ametri's requests 24 hours notice for any appointment cancellations. Guests failing to show will be charged 50% of the scheduled service.

Payment: We accept cash, checks and the following credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

Cell Phones: Out of courtesy to our other guests, we ask that cell phones be switched to silent mode and to please keep the volume of your cell phone conversations to a minimum while at D'Ametri's.

Return Policy: We are dedicated to the satisfaction of our guests. Should you find that a product you have purchased from us is not working for you, please bring it back to us within 30 days and more than half full, and we will gladly exchange it for you or issue you a D'Ametri's retail credit.

Service Policy: If you are unsatisfied with your service, please contact us. No refunds will be given for services rendered; however, the same stylist will work with you to address and remedy the challenge.
